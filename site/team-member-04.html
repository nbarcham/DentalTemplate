Kimberly Barker

MBBS, DCH, DRCOG, MRCGP

During her 20 years long career Kimberly worked for numerous dental offices in the US and Canada.

Being the head of our special, customer services unit, Kimberly does her best to welcome you and accompany your treatment with us.

She is an embodiment of our philosophy of constant customer care.

That philosophy makes us focus on a full range of services we deliver. It makes our patients feel not just healthier, but more happy and cheerful too!
